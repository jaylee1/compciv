On Wednesday, December 29, 2004, at approximately 11:15 P.M., two plainclothes vice officers were transporting an arrested person to jail in an unmarked vehicle when they were intentionally struck by another vehicle at 2400 Walnut Hill Lane. The W/M/38 suspect intentionally used his vehicle to strike the vehicle occupied by the officers while yelling that he was going to kill the officers. One officer fired his weapon at the suspect, but did not strike the suspect. The suspect was not injured. Suspect weapon was a motor vehicle. Suspect was charged with Aggravated Assault with a Motor Vehicle. No officer was injured. One officer fired 1 round. Involved Officer: A/M 9 years, 3 months service.
