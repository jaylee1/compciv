On Tuesday, October 21, 2003, at approximately 10:00 P.M., uniformed patrol officers were conducting a Knock and Talk operation at 9830 Chapel Creek Circle #107, on an apartment that was suspected of selling drugs. As officers knocked on the door a Latin Male opened and then immediately slammed the door shut. Officers entered the location when a L/M/18 suspect fired a shotgun at the officers. Two officers were struck. The suspect was not injured. Suspect weapon was a shotgun, one round was fired. Suspect was charged with two counts of Aggravated Assault Public Servant. Two officers were injured. No officers fired their weapons.
