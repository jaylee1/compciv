On Saturday, June 21, 2008, at approximately 1:15 A.M., uniformed patrol officers in marked vehicles responded to a disturbance call involving a W/M/29 suspect at 18081 Midway Road. As the officers attempted to speak to the suspect who was inside an apartment, the suspect opened the door and pointed what appeared to be a rifle at the officers. Two officers fired their weapons striking the suspect one time. The suspect was injured and transported to Parkland Hospital. Suspect weapon was a toy rifle. Suspect was charged with Aggravated Assault Public Servant. No officer was injured. Two officers fired a combined 5 rounds. Involved Officers: W/M 7 years, 2 months service, L/M 4 years, 10 months service.
