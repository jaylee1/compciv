On Friday, August 19, 2005, at approximately 11:09 A.M., members of the Dallas Police Sex Offender Apprehension team (SOAP) were in the City of Duncanville executing an arrest warrant for a W/M/22 at 500 Steeger Drive. The armed suspect fled the location towards a nearby park. Officers searched the park and located the suspect. The suspect pointed his handgun at an officer and the officer fired his weapon, striking the suspect three times. The suspect was pronounced deceased at Methodist Central Hospital. Suspect weapon was a .45 caliber semi-automatic handgun, 9 live rounds. No officer was injured. One officer fired 3 rounds. Involved Officer: W/M 29 years service.
