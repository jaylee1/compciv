On Thursday, November 20, 2003, at approximately 8:25 P.M., an off-duty police officer was driving in the 6300 block of C.F. Hawn Freeway, when two L/M suspects shot out his rear window. The officer pursued the suspects trying to get the license plate of the vehicle when the suspect pointed a gun at the officer. The officer fired his weapon striking the suspect vehicle two times. No suspect was injured. Suspect weapon was a .38 caliber revolver, 6 fired rounds. No officer was injured. One officer fired 2 rounds. Involved Officer: W/M 21 years service.
