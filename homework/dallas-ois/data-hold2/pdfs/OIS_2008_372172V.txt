On Sunday, December 7, 2008, at approximately 2:23 A.M., uniformed patrol officers driving marked vehicles were dispatched on a Family Violence disturbance call at 4791 W. Ledbetter Drive apartment 1017 involving the B/M/22 suspect. The suspect attempted to flee in a vehicle and as he fled, he attempted to strike one officer with the vehicle. Another officer thought the suspect had struck the officer and fired his weapon at the suspect missing him. The suspect was not injured. Suspect used a vehicle as a weapon. Suspect was charged with Aggravated Assault Public Servant and Assault. No officer was injured. One officer fired 7 rounds. Involved Officer: B/M 12 year, 9 months service.
