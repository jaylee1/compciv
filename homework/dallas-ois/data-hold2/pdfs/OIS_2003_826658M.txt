On Thursday, October 30, 2003, at approximately 12:10 a.m., plainclothes deployment officers were searching for stolen vehicles when they observed the L/M/19 suspect driving a stolen vehicle. While the suspect parked the vehicle at 8307 Meadow Road, officers approached the suspect. The suspect attempted to run over officers with the vehicle while attempting to flee the scene. Two officers fired their weapons at the suspect striking him two times. Suspect was injured and transported to Baylor Hospital. Suspect was charged with 4-counts of Aggravated Assault on a Public Servant with a Motor Vehicle. Two officers were injured. Two officers fired a combined total of 6 rounds. Involved Officers: L/M 8 years service, W/M 5 years service.
