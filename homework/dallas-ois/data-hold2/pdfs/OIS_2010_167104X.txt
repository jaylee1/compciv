Officers learned a B/M/25 suspect had assaulted one male and pointed a gun at another male. On Wednesday, June 15, 2014, at 12:00 A.M., officers were talking to the victim when the suspect returned to the location. The suspect fled on foot when confronted by an officer. The suspect was located at 2015 Ferndale Street where he pointed a rifle at officers. One officer fired his weapon, missing the suspect. The suspect was not injured. Suspect weapon was a SKS Rifle, 16 live rounds. Suspect was charged with Aggravated Assault Public Servant, Assault, Deadly Conduct, and Terroristic Threat. No officer was injured.
