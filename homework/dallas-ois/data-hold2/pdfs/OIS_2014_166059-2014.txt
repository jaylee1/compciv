On Thursday, July 10, 2014, at approximately 11:57 P.M., uniformed patrol officers in a marked vehicle, conducted a traffic stop at 500 S. Llewellyn Avenue. After the violator came to a stop, a L/M/33 suspect in the front passenger seat, stepped out of the vehicle and immediately fired ten shots at the officers. Bullets struck the windshield and hood of the squad car. Officers returned fire, but did not strike the suspect. The vehicle fled the location. Detectives identified the suspect and he was arrested several days later. The suspect was not injured. Suspect weapon was a .45 semi-automatic handgun which was not recovered, 10 rounds were fired at the officers. The suspect was charged with Aggravated Assault Public Servant (two cases). No officer was injured. Two officers fired a combined 5 rounds. Involved Officers: W/M 4 years, 1 month service, L/M 4 years, 1 month service.
