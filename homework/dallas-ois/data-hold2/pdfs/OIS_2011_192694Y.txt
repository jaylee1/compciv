On Friday, July 22, 2011, at approximately 1:30 A.M., uniformed patrol officers in marked vehicles responded to a burglary in progress at the Levine’s department store at 2550 Redbird Lane. One officer observed the B/M/21 suspect exit the rear of the business. The suspect had an unknown object in his hand and refused to comply with the officer’s verbal commands. The suspect turned towards the officer with the object in his hand and the officer fired his weapon, but did not strike the suspect. The suspect was injured and was transported to the hospital for injuries to his hand that occurred during the burglary. Suspect was unarmed. Suspect was charged with Burglary of a Business. No officer was injured. One officer fired 5 rounds. Involved Officer: B/M/ 2 years, 3 months service.
